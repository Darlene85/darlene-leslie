The Beginning

I am a proud graduate of Fort Lewis College in Durango, Colorado. In 2008, I graduated with a B.A., in Interdisciplinary Studies. With my degree, I was able to study law, art, education, and Native American studies. In 2009, I received a Dr. Linda Ekmann Simmon's Award for Outstanding Native American Student in Education.

The Journey

In 2011, I returned to Arizona to continue my career as an elementary teacher. While I worked with Mesa Public Schools, I became a part of the Montessori program. In 2016, I became a certified Montessori teacher accredited by the American Montessori Society. I do beleive that the Montessori Method is the best way to educate our children.

The Learning Continues

As an educator, I recognized the growing interest in technology and completed a full stack web development program with the University of Arizona in 2018. This field of study pushed me beyond my limits. I enjoyed the demand of critical thinking skills and learned anything is possible with determination.
